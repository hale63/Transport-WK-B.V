About WKTransport

Built on Trust.
Driven by
Excellence.

Founded in 1996 in the Netherlands, WKTransport has grown from a regional carrier into one of the most trusted logistics partners in Northern Europe. We combine decades of experience with modern technology to deliver fast, safe, and cost-effective transport.

Our team of over 200 professionals operates from strategic hubs across Rotterdam, Amsterdam, and Venlo, managing thousands of shipments each month across 12 European countries.

  • Full-load and part-load transport across all EU corridors
  • Real-time GPS tracking on every shipment
  • Temperature-controlled and hazardous goods handling
  • Dedicated account manager for every client
